Short BIO

I am a PhD student at Politecnico di Milano in the Image and Sound Processing Lab. I am working on audio signal processing, music information retrieval, audio forensics . I graduated in Computer Science Engineering, track Sound and Music Engineering at Politecnico di Milano. I am a piano player and a music lover.
