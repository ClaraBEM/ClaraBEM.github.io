Short BIO

I graduated in Computer Science Engineering, track Sound and Music Engineering at Politecnico di Milano. My thesis work focused on denoising algorithms for Higher Prder Ambisonics recordings. I am currently a PhD student in Politecnico di Milano at Image and Sound Processing Lab and I am working on audio signal processing and music information retrieval. I am a piano player and a music lover.
